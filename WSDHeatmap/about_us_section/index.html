Welcome to Our Project

Our goal is to explore investment income data across the United States using interactive heatmaps and timeline visualizations. This platform helps users understand how investment-based income has changed over time and across regions.

Project Overview

Using data from IPUMS, our visualizations highlight how investment income differs by ZIP code and county. Users can log in to save their preferred datasets and filters for later use.
